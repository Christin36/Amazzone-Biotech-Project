1. ZONAPT 650 [ Paracetamol Tablets IP 650 mg]

Medical Description:

P 650 MG Tablet is the most widely used over-the-counter (OTC) medication. It is used for the treatment of fever, headache, toothache, backache, nerve pain, musculoskeletal pain, etc. associated with/without fever. P 650 MG Tablet works by decreasing the intensity of pain signals to the brain. It also prevents the release of prostaglandins, the chemical that causes pain and an increase in body temperature.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent:

  • Fever
  • Mild to moderate pain
  • Post-vaccination fever

Contraindications

P 650 MG Tablet is not recommended if you are allergic to it. Inform your doctor if you have liver or kidney problems before taking this medicine. Though this medicine is safe for use in pregnancy and breastfeeding, it is recommended to consult your doctor before taking it.

Side Effects

  • Nausea and Vomiting
  • Stomach pain
  • Diarrhoea
  • Indigestion
  • Skin Rash

2. AMZER-D [ Diclofenac Potassium and Serratiopeptidase Tablets]

Medical Description:

Amzer-D Tablet 15's belongs to the class of drugs known as 'non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug' (NSAID). It is a fixed-dose combination composed of diclofenac and serratiopeptidase. Diclofenac works by blocking the action of a chemical messenger known as cyclo-oxygenase (COX) which cause pain and swelling at the injured or damaged tissue. Serratiopeptidase is a proteolytic enzyme that helps in the breakdown of insoluble protein (fibrin) a by-product of blood clots into smaller units.

Usage:

Amzer-D Tablet 15's is used in the reduction of pain and inflammation due to bone or soft tissue injury, resolution of postoperative inflammation, oedema (swollen tissue with fluid) and pain

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Allergic to Diclofenac, Serratiopeptidase or any ingredient of Amzer D tablet.
  • Bleeding from the stomach and intestine or having an active peptic ulcer.
  • Allergic to other painkillers such as aspirin
  • Heart disease like heart failure and high blood pressure.
  • Kidney and liver function.
  • Pregnant or elderly (65 years of age or older).
  • Do not take this medicine for relieving pain before, during or after cardiac bypass surgery.

Side Effects

  • Dizziness
  • Headache
  • Spinning of head
  • Stomach discomforts
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Rashes
  • Indigestion

3. ZONORAB -20 [ Rabeprazole Sodium Gastro-Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Rabeprazole sodium tablet belongs to a group of medicines called proton pump inhibitors. It reduces the amount of acid your stomach makes and relieves the pain associated with heartburn and acid reflux

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Treatment of Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Severe liver problems
  • HIV
  • Osteoporosis
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women should also consult their doctor before taking it.

Side Effects

  • Diarrhoea
  • Dizziness
  • Flatulence
  • Headache
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Sore throat
  • Stomach pain

Medical Description:

Zonorab -D include Rabeprazole (20mg), Domperidone (30mg) as active ingredients which is a sustained-release capsule. It is indicated for rapid symptomatic relief in GERD. It relives both daytime & nighttime heartburn.

5.ENTEZ -40 [ Pantoprazole Gastro- Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Pantoprazole is a proton pump inhibitor that decreases the amount of acid produced in the stomach. Pantoprazole is used to treat erosive esophagitis (damage to the oesophagus from stomach acid caused by gastroesophageal reflux disease, or GERD) in adults and children who are at least 5 years old.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

The use of this medicine is considered harmful for patients with liver problems, HIV, allergic reactions to similar medicines in the past or have osteoporosis.

Side Effects

  • Vomiting
  • Headache
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea
  • Flatulence
  • Diarrhoea
