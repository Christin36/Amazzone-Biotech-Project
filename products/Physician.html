1.LABEZON–100 [ Labetalol Tablets IP 100 mg]

Medical Description:

Labezon 100mg Tablet contains Labetalol as an active ingredient. It is a blood pressure-lowering medicine used for the treatment of high blood pressure and chest pain due to a heart condition (Angina). Labezon 100mg Tablet works by relaxing the smooth muscles of blood vessels that decrease the blood pressure and improves the blood flow. It can reduce the chances of getting a future heart attack, stroke and complications associated with high blood pressure by improving the blood pressure. Apart from taking this medicine on time regularly making certain lifestyle changes, healthy diet choices and regular exercise can be an added benefit in controlling blood pressure better.

3. MILKYZON -D [ Calcium Citrate Maleate and Vitamin D3 Tablets]

Medical Description:

Milkyzon-D tablet contains a combination of calcium and vitamin D3 (also known as cholecalciferol/cholecalciferol). Milkyzon-D tablet is used to treat vitamin D and calcium deficiency and as an adjuvant to other medicines in bone-related disorders such as osteoporosis. It is also used as a general supplement to help maintain healthy bones and teeth. This medicine should not be consumed more than the quantity prescribed by your doctor. While taking this medicine, try to incorporate food that is rich in vitamin D and calcium. Exposure to sunlight is also essential for the synthesis of vitamin D in the body.

4.VOMIZON-4 [ Ondansetron Orally Disintegrating Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Vomizon 4 tablet contains ondansetron as an active ingredient. It is an antiemetic medicine used to control nausea and vomiting. Ondanserton in Vomizon blocks the release of serotonin, a chemical in the body responsible for nausea and vomiting. Thus preventing nausea and vomiting. You should take the exact dose as prescribed by the doctor. Before using Vomizon 4, inform the doctor about your detailed medical history, including all the medicines you are currently taking.

5.ZONOFIX-200 [Cefixime Tablets I.P 200mg]

Medical Description:

Zonofix 200 tablet is used for the treatment of bacterial infections of the urinary tract, lungs, throat and airways, gallbladder and bile duct. It is also used in the treatment of typhoid fever. It is an antibiotic medicine containing cefixime as its active ingredient. Cefixime in tab Zonofix acts by inhibiting the growth of infection-causing bacteria. Take this medicine as advised by your doctor for the prescribed duration. You should take Zonofix 200 with meals to avoid stomach upset. The course of the antibiotics should be completed as per the directions given by the doctor and it should not be stopped abruptly on your own. Stopping the course on your own may lead to antibiotic resistance and recurrence of infection.

6.CITRAZON [ Disodium Hydrogen Citrate Syrup]

Medical Description:

Citrazon liquid is used for treating gout and certain kidney stones. It alkalizes urine by increasing the pH of urine. This increases the solubility of uric acid and gets it out of the body through urine. Citrazon liquid contains disodium hydrogen citrate as its active component. This liquid inhibits the production of uric acid in the body. Thereby leading to a decrease in gouty attacks and preventing kidney stones. It should be taken in doses and duration as prescribed by the doctor. Shake the bottle well, before each use. You must have plenty of water when taking the Citrazon liquid.

8.Montizon-LC Kid [ Montelukast sodium and Levocetirizine hydrochloride Dispersible Tablets]

Medical Description:

Montizon LC Kid tablet contains a combination of Levocetirizine and Montelukast. It is an antiallergic medicine. Montizon LC Kid is used to treating allergy symptoms such as runny and stuffy nose, blockage in airways, sneezing, itching, watery eyes and other allergic symptoms. This medicine blocks the release of an allergy causing substances in the body. Montizon LC Kid is specially designed for children. Disperse Montizon LC Kid tablet in a small quantity of water, and give it to your child immediately. Give this medicine to your child as advised by your doctor, preferably at night. While on this medicine, your child may feel dizzy, sleepy and drowsy. If these side effects worsen or remain persistent, visit a nearby hospital or clinic.

Montizon LC Kid Syrup is a paediatric medicine used for the management of symptoms of allergic rhinitis such as a runny & stuffy nose, blockage in the airways, sneezing, itching, watery eyes and other allergic symptoms. It is a combination of two medicines montelukast and levocetirizine. It works by blocking the production of chemicals responsible for producing the symptoms of allergy. 10. ZONORAB -20 [ Rabeprazole Sodium Gastro-Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Zonorab 20 tablet is used to relieve acidity and heartburn in conditions such as stomach and intestinal ulcers, gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome. It contains rabeprazole as its main component that belongs to the class of medicines called 'Proton pump inhibitors which are used to reduce the amount of acid produced in the stomach. Take this medicine as prescribed and for the duration it is prescribed for by your doctor. Some common side effects of Zonorab 20 tablet are headache, nausea, vomiting and stomach pain, but not everybody gets them. These side effects normally go away with time, but if you feel the side effects persist, you should consult your doctor. Inform your doctor if you are pregnant, planning a pregnancy or breastfeeding and about all the medicines and supplements you take and all the conditions or diseases you suffer from before starting the Zonorab 20 tablet.

11.ZONORAB -D [ Rabeprazole sodium with Domperidone (Sustained release) Capsules]

Medical Description:

Rabeprazole + Domperidone is used for the treatment of gastrointestinal reflux disease (GERD). It works by reducing the amount of acid produced in the stomach and improves motility in the gut. Thus, it reduces the backflow of stomach content to the food pipe. Rabeprazole + Domperidone is available in the form of tablet, capsule. Inform your doctor if you are pregnant, planning a pregnancy or breastfeeding and about all the medicines and supplements you take and all the conditions or diseases you suffer from before starting Rabeprazole + Domperidone.

12.ENTEZ -40 [ Pantoprazole Gastro- Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Entez-40 tablet is an acid-reducing medicine. It is used to treat stomach ulcers, gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and other acidity-related problems. GERD occurs due to frequent acid reflux (burning pain or discomfort in the chest). This tablet contains pantoprazole as an active ingredient. It acts by reducing the production of stomach acid. It works best when taken on an empty stomach. You should also be watchful for which are the foods and beverages that seem to induce acidity-related problems, try to consume smaller and more frequent meals, have less oily and spicy food, avoid consumption of alcohol and drink plenty of water. For more customised dietary modifications, reach out to your doctor or nutritionist. Entez-40 tablet should be taken as prescribed by the doctor and in doses and duration as prescribed. It would be best if you take this medicine as prescribed. Do not miss any dose or take it more often than prescribed by your doctor. Entez-40 is not effective in burping or hiccups.

13.ENTEZ -DSR [Pantoprazole Gastro-resistant with Domperidone Prolonged-release Capsules IP]

Medical Description:

Entoz-DSR capsule is an anti-reflux medicine. It is used to relieve the symptoms of acid reflux disease like heartburn and chest discomfort. It is a combination medicine containing pantoprazole and domperidone as active ingredients. Take this medicine as advised by your doctor at the prescribed dose and duration. You should take Entoz-DSR at least 30 mins before the food. While on this capsule, it is advised to avoid drinking caffeinated beverages like tea, coffee, soda and spicy, junk food which otherwise may aggravate your acidity. Some common side effects that you may experience are dry mouth, diarrhoea, weakness, joint and muscle pain. Long term use of this capsule may cause vitamin b12 deficiency, thus those who are already suffering from vitamin b12 deficiency should inform your doctor about the same before taking the medicine. Please inform your doctor about all your medical conditions before using the medicine.

14. ZONAPT 650 [ Paracetamol Tablets IP 650 mg]

Medical Description:

Zonapt-650 tablet is a pain-relieving medicine. It also helps reduce fever. Zonapt contains paracetamol as its active ingredient. This medicine is used for many conditions such as headache, backache, migraine, toothache, body pain etc. This medicine may be prescribed with other disease conditions such as typhoid or the common cold, to manage fever. Pain is an unpleasant feeling caused by the formation of certain chemicals in the body in response to an injury, tissue damage or disease. Zonapt-650 mg tablet works by blocking the formation of these chemicals responsible for fever and pain. This medicine usually provides symptomatic relief to the patient taking this medicine. The dose or duration of this medicine varies among individuals depending on the severity and condition it is being used for. You should not take more than 4 tablets of the Zonapt-650 in any 24 hours. Do not take it further if you feel that your pain and fever are relieved. This medicine is not meant for children under 12 years of age. Avoid taking Zonapt-650 too often and for a prolonged duration without medical advice. Also, you should not take this medicine if you are allergic to paracetamol or any ingredient of the Zonapt-650 tablet.
