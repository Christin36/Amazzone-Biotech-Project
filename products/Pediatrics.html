1.Montizon-LC Kid [ Montelukast sodium and Levocetirizine hydrochloride Dispersible Tablets]

Medical Description:

The salt combination Levocetirizine / Montelukast is used to treat allergy symptoms. Levocetirizine HCL is a 3rd generation, non-sedative antihistamine drug that helps in reducing allergy symptoms by blocking “histamine” – a substance in the human body that mainly causes allergic symptoms. Montelukast Sodium comes under a class of medications called leukotriene receptor antagonists that helps in reducing asthma symptoms & seasonal allergies.

Usage:
  • Allergic conditions
  • Hay fever
  • Ear-Round Allergies
  • Dust or Pet Allergies
  • Reddish Itchy Weals
  • Swelling in the skin

Contraindications

Montizon-LC Tablets are contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to montelukast, levocetirizine or cetirizine, or any of the excipients. Observed reactions range from urticaria to anaphylaxis.

Side Effects

  • Diarrhoea
  • Headache
  • Indigestion
  • Sore throat
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea

3.VOMIZON [ Ondansetron Oral Solution IP]

Medical Description:

Ondansetron oral solution is a 5-HT3 receptor antagonist indicated for the prevention of nausea and vomiting.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Nausea and vomiting associated with highly emetogenic cancer chemotherapy, including cisplatin greater than or equal to 50 mg/m2.
  • Nausea and vomiting associated with radiotherapy in patients.
  • Postoperative nausea and/or vomiting

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Known to have hypersensitivity (e.g., anaphylaxis) to ondansetron or any of the components of the formulation
  • Receiving concomitant apomorphine due to the risk of profound hypotension and loss of consciousness

Side Effects

  • Hypersensitivity Reactions
  • QT Prolongation
  • Serotonin Syndrome a
  • Myocardial Ischemia
  • Masking of Progressive Ileus and Gastric Distension

4. ZONORAB -20 [ Rabeprazole Sodium Gastro-Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Rabeprazole sodium tablet belongs to a group of medicines called proton pump inhibitors. It reduces the amount of acid your stomach makes and relieves the pain associated with heartburn and acid reflux.

Usage:

Used to treat

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Treatment of Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Severe liver problems
  • HIV
  • Osteoporosis
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women should also consult their doctor before taking it.

Side Effects

  • Diarrhoea
  • Dizziness
  • Flatulence
  • Headache
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Sore throat
  • Stomach pain

5.ZONORAB -D [ Rabeprazole sodium with Domperidone (Sustained release) Capsules]

Medical Description:

Zonorab -D include Rabeprazole (20mg), Domperidone (30mg) as active ingredients which is a sustained-release capsule. It is indicated for rapid symptomatic relief in GERD. It relives both daytime & nighttime heartburn.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

6.ENTEZ -40 [ Pantoprazole Gastro- Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Pantoprazole is a proton pump inhibitor that decreases the amount of acid produced in the stomach. Pantoprazole is used to treat erosive esophagitis (damage to the oesophagus from stomach acid caused by gastroesophageal reflux disease, or GERD) in adults and children who are at least 5 years old.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

The use of this medicine is considered harmful for patients with liver problems, HIV, allergic reactions to similar medicines in the past or have osteoporosis.

Side Effects

  • Vomiting
  • Headache
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea
  • Flatulence
  • Diarrhoea
