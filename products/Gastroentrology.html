1.ZONORAB -20 [ Rabeprazole Sodium Gastro-Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Rabeprazole sodium tablet belongs to a group of medicines called proton pump inhibitors. It reduces the amount of acid your stomach makes and relieves the pain associated with heartburn and acid reflux.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Treatment of Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Severe liver problems
  • HIV
  • Osteoporosis
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women should also consult their doctor before taking it.

Side Effects

  • Diarrhoea
  • Dizziness
  • Flatulence
  • Headache
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Sore throat
  • Stomach pain

2.ZONORAB -D [ Rabeprazole sodium with Domperidone (Sustained release) Capsules]

Medical Description:

Zonorab -D include Rabeprazole (20mg), Domperidone (30mg) as active ingredients which is a sustained-release capsule. It is indicated for rapid symptomatic relief in GERD. It relieves both daytime & nighttime heartburn.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Acidity
  • Heartburn

Contraindications

Before taking the medicine always consult your doctor if you are pregnant, breastfeeding or if you are suffering from any severe stomach, liver, kidney disease, or any known disease.

Side Effects

  • Stomach pain
  • Diarrhoea
  • Constipation
  • Dryness in mouth
  • Headache
  • Dizziness

3.ENTEZ -40 [ Pantoprazole Gastro- Resistant Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Pantoprazole is a proton pump inhibitor that decreases the amount of acid produced in the stomach. Pantoprazole is used to treat erosive esophagitis (damage to the oesophagus from stomach acid caused by gastroesophageal reflux disease, or GERD) in adults and children who are at least 5 years old.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux)
  • Peptic ulcer disease

Contraindications

The use of this medicine is considered harmful for patients with liver problems, HIV, allergic reactions to similar medicines in the past or have osteoporosis.

Side Effects

  • Vomiting
  • Headache
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea
  • Flatulence
  • Diarrhoea

4.ENTEZ -DSR [Pantoprazole Gastro-resistant with Domperidone Prolonged-release Capsules IP]

Medical Description:

Main constituents of ENTEZ -DSR are Pantoprazole & Domperidone. Pantoprazole Tablets help in reducing the amount of acid the stomach makes and for treating problems like heartburn, acid reflux, and gastroesophageal reflux disease (GORD). Domperidone is a prokinetic that helps in controlling vomiting and movement of the intestine that allows the food to move easily and throw the food pipe to the stomach.

Usage:

Used to treat

  • Motion sickness
  • Ulcer
  • Nausea & Vomiting
  • Heartburn
  • Esophagitis
  • Gastroparesis

Contraindications

  • Allergy
  • Blockage
  • Osteoporosis
  • Kidney Diseases
  • Heart problems
  • Electrolyte disturbance

5.VOMIZON-4 [ Ondansetron Orally Disintegrating Tablets IP]

Medical Description:

Ondansetron orally disintegrating tablets are a 5-HT3 receptor antagonist indicated for the prevention of nausea and vomiting.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Nausea and vomiting associated with highly emetogenic cancer chemotherapy, including cisplatin greater than or equal to 50 mg/m2.
  • Nausea and vomiting associated with radiotherapy in patients.
  • Postoperative nausea and/or vomiting

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Known to have hypersensitivity (e.g., anaphylaxis) to ondansetron or any of the components of the formulation
  • Receiving concomitant apomorphine due to the risk of profound hypotension and loss of consciousness

Side Effects

  • Hypersensitivity Reactions
  • QT Prolongation
  • Serotonin Syndrome
  • Myocardial Ischemia
  • Masking of Progressive Ileus and Gastric Distension

6.ZONOFIX-200 [Cefixime Tablets I.P 200mg]

Medical Description:

Cefixime 200 belongs to the cephalosporins group of antibiotics which are considered to be bactericidal (bacterial killing ability). Cephalosporins disrupt the bacterial cell wall which is important for the integrity of the bacterial cell membrane thus killing the bacteria and checking its growth.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Infections of ear, throat, tonsils etc
  • Gonorrhea
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Infections of the skin and soft tissue
  • Tonsillitis
  • Pharyngitis
  • Bronchitis

Contraindications

This medication should not be used if you have certain medical conditions.

  • Allergic to Cefixime 200
  • Allergic to drugs belonging to cephalosporin family-like cefaclor, cefepime, cefuroxime etc
  • History of penicillin allergy
  • Kidney disease or on dialysis
  • Liver diseases
  • Colitis

Side Effects

  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Indigestion
  • Severe stomach pain
  • Diarrhea
  • Dizziness

7.CITRAZON [ Disodium Hydrogen Citrate Syrup]

Medical Description:

Disodium Hydrogen Citrate is a salt of citric acid. It is a urine alkalizer. It works by increasing the pH of urine which makes it less acidic. This helps the kidneys get rid of excess uric acid, thereby preventing gout and certain types of kidney stones.

Usage:

Used to treat or prevent

  • Constipation
  • Gastric problems
  • Acidity
  • Abdominal gas
  • Gastric complications

Contraindications

Citrazon is contraindicated in patients with severe renal impairment and associated oliguria, azotemia or anuria, untreated Addison's disease, acute dehydration, heat cramps, and severe myocardial damage. In addition, sodium salts are contraindicated in patients on a sodium-restricted diet.

Side Effects

  • Diarrhea
  • Abdominal cramps
  • Biliousness
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
